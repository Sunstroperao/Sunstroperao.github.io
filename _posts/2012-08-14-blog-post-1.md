&emsp;&emsp;Oct 29, 2023, I attended the 2023 7th CAA International Conference on Vehicular Control and Intelligence (CVCI) and was honored to be shortlisted for the best paper. The paper is titled "**Motion Query-based Multimodal Vehicle Trajectory Prediction for Autonomous Driving**". 

&emsp;&emsp;At the conference, I presented the paper and shared my research with the audience. The paper proposes a novel multimodal vehicle trajectory prediction method based on motion queries. The method can predict the future trajectory of vehicles in complex traffic scenarios, which is crucial for autonomous driving systems. The proposed method has been validated on real-world datasets and shows promising results. I am grateful for the recognition and feedback from the conference attendees. I will continue to work on autonomous driving research and contribute to the development of intelligent transportation systems.
