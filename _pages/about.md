💥 Hi, I'm Hao Jiang, a Ph.D. student in the Institue of Intelligent Automobile at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by [Prof. Xi Zhang](http://me.sjtu.edu.cn/teacher_directory1/2040.html) and [Assoc Prof. Chuan Hu](https://sites.google.com/site/chuanhumac/home). Before that, I received my M.S. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2024 and B.S. degree in from Wuhan University of Technology in 2021. 

🚀 My research interests include **perception, prediction, and planning for autonomous driving**. I am also interested in the application of **Large Language Models (LLMs)** in autonomous driving.
